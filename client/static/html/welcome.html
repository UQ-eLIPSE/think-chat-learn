Steps on using MOOCchat

  1. Answer a question

    You'll be asked to respond to a concept-related question, as well as provide a confidence rating in your ability to explain the concept.

    Make sure you keep track of the timer for each section, which is visible to the left.

  2. Discuss as a group and arrive at a best answer

    After a brief wait, you'll be placed into a group chat to discuss other student answers.

    You should arrive at what the group believes is the best answer upon conclusion of the chat.

  3. Reflect on how you changed your thinking

    You'll be asked to then provide a reflection of whether your answer changed and how.

    A second confidence rating also forms part of this.

  4. Complete a brief survey

    Before you complete the activity, we will ask a few questions about your MOOCchat experience.
