How to use MOOCchat

  1. Answer a question

    • Read, watch and answer the question presented
    • Score yourself on your confidence in explaining the concept
    • Make sure to also keep track of the timer in the left sidebar

  2. Discuss as a group and agree on a best answer

    • You'll be put into a group of up to 4 students
    • Discuss the question answers from you and other students
    • Your group must agree on a best answer before the chat ends

  3. Reflect on how you changed your thinking

    • Reflect on whether your answer changed, and how
    • Score yourself again on your confidence in explaining the concept

  4. Complete a brief survey and finish

    • Fill out a survey about your MOOCchat experience
    • You'll be presented a receipt of completion at the end for you to save or print
