Before we begin...

MOOCchat is an innovative, online peer-assisted learning tool designed to place students in small groups to engage discussion around high-level questions.

As part of research into the benefits of online chats in peer-based student learning, we require permission for the collection of your MOOCchat session data, such as your answers and discussion messages.

All information will remain completely confidential, and you may request to withdraw your consent at any time.

Please review the , which provides further information about MOOCchat and the study.

Please note that your consent or lack thereof does not impact on your ability to use MOOCchat as part of course assessment.
